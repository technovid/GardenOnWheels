Mobile Garden for the displaced villagers

Our goal is to reduce the number of Bangladeshi villagers suffering from acute hunger and other common diseases associated with malnutrition.

About

The "Garden on Wheels" initiative was started when I visited Bangladesh and researched the impact of climate change in Bangladesh. Many villagers in Bangladesh suffer from preventable diseases such as Malnutrition, Diarrhea, and Dengue fever, which are directly related to coliform pollution due to global warming. So, to alleviate malnutrition for displaced villagers affected by damaged farmland and polluted waters, I worked with the "Leaders of Today Northwest" to propose my "Garden on Wheels" initiative.
